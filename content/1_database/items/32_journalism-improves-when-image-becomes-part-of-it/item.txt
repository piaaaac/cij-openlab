Description: After years of faithful service at NRC Next, Sterre Sprengers has been working as Chief Image Editor for De Correspondent for three and a half years already. De Correspondent is a Dutch platform for independent daily news. At De Correspondent together with like-minded designers, journalists and developers Sprengers strives to the development of contemporary and international journalism. She has been involved since day one. Our meeting takes place in VolksHotel, the former head office of De Volkskrant in Amsterdam. While crowded, we have a many-sided conversation about De Correspondent's position and relevantie en possibilities of image within journalism.
